Here goes all the steps of full body challenge!
You should continue this for 1 month
20 minitues per day

Step-1:Incline Push up

  1. Stand facing the bench, table, or the edge of a bed.
  2. Place your hands on the edge of the bench just slightly wider than shoulder width.
  3. Bend your elbows to slowly lower your chest to the edge of the bench while inhaling.
  4. Push your body away from the bench until your elbows are extended
  5. Keep going with slow, steady repetitions.

Step-2:Widearm Push Up

  1. Begin in your top of a push-up position with your arms straight
  2. Step each hand a few inches wider.
  3. As you inhale, bend your elbows out to the sides as you lower your torso toward the floor.
  4. Complete as many reps as you can with proper form.

Step-3:TRICEP DIP

  1. Sit on the edge of the chair and grip the edge next to your hips.
  2. Lower yourself until your elbows
  3. Slowly push yourself back up to the start position and repeat.
  4. Begin with 3 sets of 10 repetitions and increase your sets

Step-4:Mountain Climber

  1. Get into a plank position, making sure to distribute your weight
  2. Pull your right knee into your chest as far as you can.
  3. Switch legs, pulling one knee out and bringing the other knee in.
  4. Keep your hips down, run your knees in and out.
