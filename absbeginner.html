Here goes all the steps of full body challenge!
You should continue this for 1 month
20 minitues per day

Step-1:JUMPING JACKS

  1. Stand upright with your legs together, arms at your sides.
  2. Bend your knees slightly, and jump into the air.
  3. As you jump, spread your legs to be about shoulder-width apart.
  4. Stretch your arms out and over your head.
  5. Jump back to starting position.
  6. Repeat it.

Step-2:ABDOMINAL CRUNCHES

  1. Lie down on the floor on your back and bend your knees, placing your hands behind your head .
  2. Pull your belly button towards your spine in preparation for the movement.
  3. Slowly contract your abdominals, bringing your shoulder blades about 1 or 2 inches off the floor.
  4. Hold at the top of the movement for a few seconds, breathing continuously.
  5. Slowly lower back down, but don't relax all the way.

Step-3:PLANK

  1. Your elbows are directly under your shoulders and your forearms are facing forward.
  2. Engage your abdominal muscles, drawing your navel toward your spine.
  3. Hold this position for 10 seconds. Release to floor
  4. Over time work up to 30, 45, or 60 seconds.

Step-4:RUSSIAN TWIST

  1. press your feet into the floor or extend them straight out as you get a feel for the movement.
  2. As you twist, keep your arms parallel to the floor or reach down to tap the floor beside you.
  3. Maintain a straight spine, and avoid slouching or rounding your spine.
  4. Allow your gaze to follow the movement of your hands.

Step-5:COBRA STRETCH

  1. Place your hands on the ground beneath and at the level of both shoulders.
  2. Keep your both legs contracted firmly to the ground, apart, plantarflexed.
  3. Contract and hold your abdominal muscles during the exercise.
  4. Keep raising your back until you reach the full stretch of your spine as much as you can.
  5. Hold for 30 seconds then repeat 3 to 5 times.
  6. Repeat it.
