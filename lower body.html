Here goes all the steps of full body challenge!
You should continue this for 1 month
20 minitues per day

Step-1:JUMPING JACKS

  1. Stand upright with your legs together, arms at your sides.
  2. Bend your knees slightly, and jump into the air.
  3. As you jump, spread your legs to be about shoulder-width apart.
  4. Stretch your arms out and over your head.
  5. Jump back to starting position.
  6. Repeat it.

Step-2:SQUATS

  1. Keep your feet slightly wider than shoulder-width. Straighten your back. Angle your feet slightly outward toward 10 and 2 o'clock.
  2. Pretend as though you are going to sit back in a chair. Keep your heels on the ground.
  3. As you go down, push your hips back.Go as low as your body can while still keeping your shins vertical and your heels on the ground.
  4. You can aim for 15-30 reps each set. Do one to three sets. Remember to rest between sets.

Step-3:BACKWARD LUNGE

  1. Stand tall with your feet hip distance apart.
  2. Take a large step backward and lower your body toward the floor. Both legs should be bent at a 90-degree angle at the bottom of the lunge. Rise back to start and repeat.
  3. Targets: glutes, quads, hamstrings.

Step-4:LEFT QUAD STRETCH

  1. Stand tall with your feet hip-width apart, pull your abdominals in, and relax your shoulders.
  2. Bend your left leg, bringing your heel toward your butt, and grasp your left foot with your right hand.
  3. Switch legs and repeat the stretch.
  4. Repeat it.
